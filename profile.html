PROFILE:

  Established in the year 2017, at Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, India, we"Shri Bhavani Plastic,” are leading Manufacturer, Exporter and Supplier of a wide array of Plastic Cloth Hangers, Bottom Hangers, Multi Pack Hangers, Plastic Top Hangers With Metal Hook, Hanger Hooks and many more. Available in durable construction finish and innovative designs, these are suitable to be used for use with stitched apparels, garments, dressing accessories and textiles. Motivated towards closely following up-to-date technology and global quality standards both in service and production stages, we ensure the best possible end solutions are provided to our quality conscious customers.

  Our business operations are led by experienced management who make use of their process & product knowledge to guide the team in delivering best possible end solutions. As leading plastic hanger manufacturers operating from India, the support of modern production facilities also allows us to deliver end solutions in both standard as well as customized finishes so as to perfectly match up with the end product requirements of the customers.

  Under the visionary guidance of our mentor, 'Mr. G.SELVAM', we have climbed the ladder of success. Presently, we have with us a rich inventory comprising more than 150 plastic hanger molds that can be used for stylishly hanging end products like T-shirts, boys wear, men’s wear, skirts, trousers, kids wear, home textiles, leather belts, ladies wear, curtains and others. Further, our rich experience of developing hanger molds spanning more than a decade and modern tool room facilities also allow us to offer personalized end solutions including options of hangers with company logos support in a short time and at competitive costs.
